Privacy Policy (GDPR)

Last updated: October 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AirSense, with headquarters/contact details at restlessstone18@gmail.com and project page at https://github.com/mihail-pop/airsense, (hereinafter referred to as "we" or the "Controller"), collects, uses, and protects your personal data, in accordance with the provisions of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).

The privacy of your data is of utmost importance to us. We process data only for the purpose of improving the user experience of the application and providing the requested results, without using it for commercial purposes or for training artificial intelligence models.

1. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect the data you provide directly to us, necessary to create your account and use the application's features:

2. Purposes of Data Processing

We process your data exclusively for the following purposes:

  1. Account Management: Creation, authentication, and administration of your user account.
  2. Application Functionality and User Experience (UX): Using data (e.g., location, inputs) to provide the basic features of the application and to improve how you interact with it.
  3. Providing Results (AI System): Your inputs are processed by an artificial intelligence system to generate the requested results. ATTENTION: This AI system is configured NOT to store, learn from, or train on user data or inputs. Data is processed instantly to generate the response and is not retained for future training.
  4. Technical Support: Responding to your support requests and troubleshooting technical issues.

We DO NOT use your data for commercial purposes, direct marketing, detailed profiling, or for selling them to third parties.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds, according to Art. 6 and Art. 9 of the GDPR:

  • Performance of a Contract: Processing is necessary to provide you with the application's services, according to the accepted Terms and Conditions.
  • Consent (for special data): Processing of health-related data (e.g., allergies) is done exclusively based on your explicit and voluntary consent. You can withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the legality of processing carried out before withdrawal.
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing of usage data (anonymized, where possible) to ensure the proper functioning, security, and continuous improvement of the service (UX).

4. Data Storage

  • Storage Duration: Personal data is stored only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes mentioned above or until your request for deletion (exercise of the right to be forgotten).
  • Data Security: We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction.

5. Data Subject Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you benefit from the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request the correction of inaccurate data or the completion of incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be forgotten"): You can request the deletion of your data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request the restriction of processing of your data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and you have the right to transmit it to another controller, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw it at any time.
